# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

EduBoost SA exists to give every South African child access to quality, personalised education — regardless of background, language, or circumstance. Our community reflects that mission. We pledge to make participation in this project a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free experience for everyone.

We commit to acting and interacting in ways that build an open, inclusive community where contributors of all experience levels, backgrounds, and identities feel valued.

---

## Our Standards

**Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment:**

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of different viewpoints and experiences, including those from different South African cultural and linguistic backgrounds
- Giving and graciously accepting constructive feedback
- Accepting responsibility when we make mistakes, and focusing on how to improve
- Showing empathy toward other community members, especially beginners
- Acknowledging the mission: everything we build is for real children and their families

**Examples of unacceptable behaviour:**

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments of any kind
- Sexist, racist, or otherwise discriminatory language or imagery
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Trolling or deliberately inflammatory comments in issues, PRs, or discussions
- Any conduct that could harm the reputation of the project or its users (including vulnerable learners)
- Advocating for, or encouraging, any of the above behaviours

---

## Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying the standards of acceptable behaviour and taking appropriate corrective action in response to any instances of unacceptable behaviour.

Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces — GitHub Issues, Pull Requests, code reviews, Discussions, and any other official project communication channels. It also applies when an individual is officially representing the project in public spaces.

---

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the project maintainer via the contact method listed on the [GitHub profile](https://github.com/NkgoloL).

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. The maintainer is obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter of any incident.

**Enforcement steps:**

1. **Warning** — a private, written warning with clarification of the violation
2. **Temporary ban** — a temporary suspension from contributing to the project
3. **Permanent ban** — permanent exclusion from all project spaces

The severity and circumstance of the violation will determine which step applies.
